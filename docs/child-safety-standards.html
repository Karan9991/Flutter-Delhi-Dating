Delhi Dating — Child Safety Standards

Delhi Dating is an adults-only platform (18+) and maintains zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Our standards

  • Users must be at least 18 years old.
  • Any CSAE/CSAM content or behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Attempts to groom minors or request sexual content are prohibited.
  • Impersonation, coercion, trafficking, and exploitation are prohibited.

In-app reporting and enforcement

  • Users can report profiles and chats in-app.
  • Users can immediately block abusive or suspicious accounts.
  • Reported accounts may be restricted, suspended, or permanently removed.
  • We preserve relevant evidence according to legal and platform requirements.

Law enforcement compliance

Delhi Dating complies with applicable child safety laws and reports relevant CSAE/CSAM matters to appropriate regional or national authorities where required.
