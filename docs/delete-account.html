Your Delhi Dating account has been deleted

This page explains what data was removed and what may remain for other participants.

Effective immediately

What we deleted

  • Account (Firebase Authentication user)
  • Profile document and fields (display name, photo, age/geo flags)
  • Availability (your bus route, time window, slot keys, day key)
  • Blocks you created (users/{uid}/blocks/*)
  • Matches that involved your account (matches/*)
  • Messages you sent in chats
  • Avatar file from storage (avatars/{uid}.jpg)
  • FCM tokens registered for push notifications (users/{uid}/fcmTokens/*)

What may remain

  • Other participants’ messages in chats you were part of
  • Chat rooms you were in may continue to exist for the other person (your membership was removed)
  • Aggregate logs or backups maintained by platform providers (Firebase/Google) for limited periods

Can this be undone?

No. Account deletion is permanent. You can create a new account anytime and set your preferences again.
